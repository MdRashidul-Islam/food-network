Spatchcock Chicken

The term “spatchcock” is derived from an old Irish abbreviation of “dispatching the cock”. It’s a cooking method involving cutting out the backbone of the chicken so it cooks flat, resulting in a faster, more evenly cooked chicken with crispier skin.
